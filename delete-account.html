Account & Data Deletion

If you have a Raith account in Lifelink, you can delete your account and all associated cloud data at any time. This page explains how to request deletion and what data is affected.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete from within the app

  1. Open Lifelink on your device
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon in the top right)
  3. Scroll to the Account section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, send an email to the address below with the subject line "Delete my Lifelink account" and include the email address associated with your Raith account. We will process your request and confirm deletion.

Send deletion requests to:

googleplay@raith.dev

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our cloud servers (Firebase):

Data Type Action Timeframe
Email address & authentication credentials Deleted Immediately
Player profiles & commander lists Deleted Within 30 days
Game history & statistics Deleted Within 30 days
Playgroup configurations Deleted Within 30 days
Deck import preferences Deleted Within 30 days
Local data on your device Retained Until you uninstall the app or clear app data

Local data (stored on your device) is not affected by account deletion. To remove local data, uninstall the app or clear its data through your device's settings.

No data is retained on our servers after deletion is complete. There is no additional retention period beyond the 30-day processing window.

No Account?

If you use Lifelink without a Raith account, all your data is stored locally on your device only. No data is sent to any server, and there is nothing to delete on our end. Simply uninstall the app to remove all data.
