Lifelink
Privacy Policy

Lifelink is a Magic: The Gathering Commander life tracking app for Android, developed by Raith. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

What We Collect

Lifelink collects only the data necessary to provide its features. There are two modes of use:

Without an account (local only):

  • Player names, life totals, commander damage, counters, and game history
  • Commander and deck preferences, including commander names
  • Archidekt or Moxfield usernames if you use deck import

All of this stays on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.

With a Raith account (optional cloud sync):

  • Your email address and password, used for authentication
  • The same game and preference data listed above, synced to the cloud

Lifelink does not collect device identifiers, location data, analytics, advertising IDs, or access your contacts, photos, or files. There are no ads in the app.

Third-Party Services

When you create a Raith account, the app uses these services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) handles account sign-in. Your password is stored using industry-standard hashing; Raith never sees or stores your password directly.
  • Google Cloud Firestore stores your synced game data. Data is encrypted at rest on Google infrastructure.

The app also uses the Scryfall API (scryfall.com) to fetch card artwork for display. No user data is sent to Scryfall; the app only requests card images and search results.

How Your Data Is Protected

  • All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Cloud data is encrypted at rest on Google infrastructure
  • Passwords are hashed by Firebase Authentication, never stored in plain text
  • Your data is never sold, traded, or shared with any third party

Data Retention and Deletion

Local data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear its data through your device settings.

Cloud data (if you have a Raith account) can be deleted in two ways:

  • From within the app: Settings > Account > Delete Account
  • By emailing googleplay@raith.dev

When you delete your account, all associated cloud data is permanently removed within 30 days of the request.

Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including the GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete your data and account entirely

Firebase and Google Cloud act as data processors on behalf of Raith. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at googleplay@raith.dev.

Children's Privacy

Lifelink is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at googleplay@raith.dev and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at googleplay@raith.dev.
